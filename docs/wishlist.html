What I *Think* I Want To Learn Next

If you're anything like me, you find yourself going down the rabbit hole of "what should I try out/learn next" A LOT. And unfortunately, it happens so easily because it's never just "learn X". Nah, it's "learn X, but first, we need to cover Y & Z". Or more commonly, in the middle of your journey, "to do X we must use Y"; sounds typical, except you have no freaking idea that Y was even a thing until 2-5 seconds ago. You can always choose to skim the deeper details to get the job done, but inevitably, you'll be forced encouraged to better understand Y and Z, so you can fix something that went wrong with X—it's fun, honestly, in a masochist sorta way ¯\_(ツ)_/¯.

Still, I thought it would be cool to create a list of things (in no particular order, mind you) that I wish to spend time with in the future. It'll give me a visible list that I could see so I may actually remmeber everything, and it can possibly give any fellow devs on their self-taught journey ideas on what to do next, so why not?

Oh, and there will be links. Enjoy!
